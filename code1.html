Who am I as a Poet?

Marc Aaron Manansala is currently taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at 
Batangas State University-Alangilan Campus. A writer and spoken word artist known as Habagat 
who performs in different events and competitions. He is the President and Founder of 
Istilo Poetry - Oriental Mindoro. He is the one behind the facebook page, 
"Habagat's Untold Feelings". He has received various awards and recognitions in the 
field of spoken word poetry, such as I-Tech's Spoken Word Poetry Champion 2018. He also 
performed in MAHALTA Resorts and Convention Center's province-wide Spoken Word Poetry 
Contest, 2019, where he was awarded as the champion for the 2nd round and 3rd place in the 
Grand Finals. A winner of the spoken word poetry category at the ICP-Youth Ministry
Summer Sports Festival 2019 and a grand-finalist in the Southern Luzon Etibakbakan Poetry
Slam 2020. Festival of Faith 2021 - Vicariate Level Champion and First Runner up in 
Diocesan Level in Spoken Word category.
Aside from being a writer and performer, he also took part as a spoken word poetry judge in 
different schools and universities, such as Isabela State University-Cauayan Campus and
Divine Word College of Calapan, and in the 4th Philippine EcoFestival 2020, a nationwide wide.


He was also invited to partake as a guest performer in DWOM-FM 105.5. He is the director of
the drama series, "Ma, Pa. May Sasabihin Po ako. Pakinggan Po Ninyo." under the
MAPA Productions of BatStateU-Dulaang Batangan.
